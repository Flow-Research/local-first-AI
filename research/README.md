# Research

Use this folder for reading plans and paper notes.

Research should lead to design choices, not just summaries.

## Folder Meaning

| Folder | Meaning |
|---|---|
| `paper-notes/` | Notes from individual papers or technical sources |
| `reading-lists/` | Monthly or topic-based reading lists |
| `learning-logs/` | Human reflections on what was learned, what AI helped with, and what was personally verified |

## Paper Note Template

```markdown
# Paper Note: Title

## Source

Link:

## Why I Read It

## Main Idea

## What Matters For This Project

## Design Question It Raises

## Possible Experiment

## Decision Or Follow-Up
```
